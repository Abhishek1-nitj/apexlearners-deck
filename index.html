Roles We Train For

MIS Executive

Builds MS Excel and Power BI dashboards, automates daily and weekly reports, and maintains data accuracy across departments.

Data Analyst

Cleans and analyzes business data using SQL and Excel, builds dashboards in Power BI or Tableau, and shares insights with stakeholders.

Reporting Analyst

Prepares recurring MIS and management reports, tracks KPIs, and ensures timely, error-free reporting to leadership.

Operations Executive

Monitors day-to-day process execution, tracks SLAs and TAT, and resolves escalations across teams.

Business Analyst

Gathers business requirements, documents processes, and works with tech and operations teams to improve workflows.

CRM Executive

Manages customer data in CRM tools like Salesforce or Zoho, tracks leads and follow-ups, and reports on retention metrics.

Finance Executive

Handles invoicing, vendor reconciliation, and basic bookkeeping using Tally or Excel; supports monthly closing activities.

HR Executive

Manages candidate sourcing, screening calls, interview scheduling, and onboarding coordination.

Customer Support / Process Associate

Handles voice and non-voice customer queries, logs tickets in CRM systems, and ensures resolution within defined SLAs.
